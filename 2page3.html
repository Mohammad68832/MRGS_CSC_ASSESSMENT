Statistics of Pollution

Since the 1950’s, statisticians have been recording the amount of pollution in our environment per decade. Due to “Our World In Data”, we can analyse the statistics and effects of pollution

The production of plastic is defined as the sum of all plastics produced over a specific period of time. As such, it is measured in all plastics produced per decade.

Plastic waste that flows into the ocean, damages the environment and kills the animals. In 2019, the global rate was around 6 million tonnes of plastic waste emitted into the ocean. This is roughly 1.3% of plastics produced in that year. This then accelerates even more per year , with about 15 million tonnes of plastic entering the ocean each year.

Throughout history, the ocean has been a very important treasure that it was very important to take care of. It was for food, relaxation, transportation, and many other things. However, nowadays it is used as a dumping ground for all our waste plastics. This is one of the many things that shows the contrast between our respect for the environment in the past, and in the present.
